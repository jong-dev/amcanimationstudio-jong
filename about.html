Who we are

AMC Animation Studio is an animation business based in the Philippines that produces high-quality animated
content, mainly but not limited to 3D visual development (projection mapping, music videos, motion graphics,
product animation), 3D animation, 3D cinematic trailers (title sequences, game trailers, movie trailers),
NFT production, and game environment design.

We are dedicated to bringing you the most exciting, creative, and compelling visuals available today. Our team
creates visual content that ensures your message remains clear to your target audience. We manage, direct,
and produce your unique projects from scratch, starting with concept development to the most complicated
and versatile execution that will meet your business's needs.

We believe that every project demands a unique approach to stand out in today's visual content, so let's work
together to make your project a success!
